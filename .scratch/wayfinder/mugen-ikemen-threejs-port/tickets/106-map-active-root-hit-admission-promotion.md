# Map Active-root Hit-admission Promotion

Type: research
Status: ready
Blocked by: None

## Goal

Map pinned IKEMEN multi-root attacker/getter ordering and current pair combat ownership before admitting P3-P8 to direct hit detection.

## Acceptance

- Pin run-order attacker/getter eligibility, team policy, Clsn, priority, guard-distance, target, and mutation ordering.
- Audit current pair direct-combat, target, guard, hit-override, reversal, contact, and presentation owners.
- Define an actor-candidate read model independent from snapshot/presentation/body-push diagnostics.
- Separate hit admission from mutation, targets, effects, round, resources, HUD/audio, and helper/projectile combat.
- Cover disabled, standby, over-KO, same-side, no Clsn, HitBy/NotHitBy, pause, and simultaneous contacts.
- Produce implementation-ready contract, adversarial audit, deletion criteria, roadmap sync, and commit.

## Claim Ceiling

Allowed: source-backed active-root direct-hit admission plan.

Blocked: runtime combat widening, mutation, targets, throws, helpers/projectiles, score movement, or full parity.
