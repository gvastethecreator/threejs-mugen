# Map Active-root Direct-hit Mutation

Type: research
Status: ready
Blocked by: None

## Goal

Map the smallest source-backed transaction that can promote one admitted active-root direct contact into real hit/guard mutation without corrupting pair-owned targets, effects, round, resources, or presentation.

## Acceptance

- Pin simultaneous-contact, priority, guard, HitOverride, ReversalDef, juggle, target, and mutation order.
- Audit current resolver state/effect/target/resource ownership for non-P1/P2 actors.
- Define plural opponent selection and actor-keyed mutation stores.
- Preserve deterministic pair behavior and read-only admission diagnostics.
- Cover reserve attacker/getter, same-side policy, KO, pause/hitpause, repeated contacts, and reset.
- Produce implementation-ready contract, adversarial audit, deletion criteria, roadmap sync, and commit.

## Claim Ceiling

Allowed: source-backed active-root direct-hit mutation plan.

Blocked: runtime mutation widening, helpers/projectiles, throws, round/HUD/audio parity, score movement, or full parity.
