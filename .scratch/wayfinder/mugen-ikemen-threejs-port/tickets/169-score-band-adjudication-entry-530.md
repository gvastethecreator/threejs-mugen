CC0 journey provides a legal loader/runtime/browser envelope, and the official
Training Room stage route adds optional-private stage/package evidence through
the real ZIP input, Studio Stage, browser QA, and native closeout.

## Limits

This is a minimum band threshold, not public breadth. The local KFM fixture,
optional-private stage sample, exact VM/Common1 timing, full stage parity,
commercial redistribution, and all full-engine claims remain bounded or
blocked.
